Rounding Borders/Corners with CSS (Border-Radius)

How to round a corner:

Modifying the roundness of borders and corners with CSS is super easy! Your best friend in this process is the border-radius property. You can individually change each corner by identifying the desired corner with this property (ex. border-top-right-radius). Each corner can have it's own unique radius, or a general radius can be applied if the corner is not identified. The order goes from top left to top right to bottom right to bottom left.
